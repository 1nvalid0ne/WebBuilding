About Me!

Who Am I?

So who am I really? I am a social shut-in that loves my GBA emulators and everything to do with the digital world. I've been running around the computer ever since I was able to get my hands on one. 5th grade to be exact. For all thise years, the way computers worked have ALWAYS fascinated me.

My Adventures

My adventures with computers will hopefully never end. It started with the Windows file explorer and just looking through each and every file. Then, eventually finding command prompt and learning how to create and write batch programs.

Batch was the first coding language that I learned about, and I used it to make programs and software in the console. I know, not very impressive, but it opened up a ton of realizations for me. I realized that I could easily sit in front of a computer all day and be completely mesmerized by how it works. That the computer was my favorite thing in the world while I was infront of it.

Okay, okay, I'll hurry up. I am taking a Java course in the high-school I am currently attending, and teaching myself HTML, CSS, JS, and PHP in my own time. I feel like I've grown a lot since I started, and I've really started to throttle up on my obsession recently (ever since Christmas of 2020). I hope to do this for the rest of my life.

Plans for The Future

Currently, I wish to go into fullstack development. I know, it's a big title. Front-end and back-end development?! I just absolutey love making and designing things on the computer, money is just a plus to it.

Besides this website, I am preparing to collaborate with a tech-savvy friend of mine on creating a forum website! There are links for more info on that around the site, under 'Jump To...'.

Along with the forum site I plan to make, start learning C# on Unity and 3D modeling w/ Blender within the next 2-3 years. No. I don't want to do be a professional game dev, because they're treated like crapp. But it would be cool to make a few games, whether they're 3D or 2D.
